A football club, the right to vote and Star Wars. Three elements that firstly have nothing in common, but one thing unites us, feminism. In great expansion in recent years, feminism has become very important in today's society. ndeed we can regularly see feminist actions coming from groups or even from a simple gathering. But there is one thing not to forget, the road to get there was long and many fights were necessary. One of the most important is undoubtedly the one for women's right to vote which started many years ago and is still not finished in some countries. Many disciplines have also had their importance in the feminist struggle, one of them is the cinema and besides the main subject of this site.
