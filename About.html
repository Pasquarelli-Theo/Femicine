About me

I’m a student at BUT MMI in Montbéliard named Théo Pasquarelli and I’m 18. It’s project is very important to me because it is the first one I have done, and I thank you for consulting it. I arrived in MMI in September 2021 after a long search and I do not regret my choice ! This site deals with feminism, a very important subject because despite our time there are still many inequalities between men and women. I decided to approach feminism in large part through the cinema because it is one of my passions but also because many actors and actresses claim to be feminist and there are a lot of movie on the subject.
